Solar System Model

Science Physics Astronomy

This scaled model accurately represents the relative sizes and distances between planets in our solar system. The project includes a mechanical system that demonstrates planetary rotation and revolution with correct relative speeds. The model helps visualize the vast scale of our solar system and the dynamics of planetary motion.

Key Features

  • 1:10 billion scale model showing accurate relative sizes and distances
  • Motorized system demonstrating planetary movements
  • LED lights representing stars in the background
  • Information panels with key facts about each planet
Teacher's Feedback:

"An exceptional project that clearly demonstrates understanding of astronomical scales. The attention to detail in both the physical construction and the explanatory materials is outstanding."
- Ms. Johnson, Astronomy Teacher

Completed: October 2022

Fractal Geometry Exploration

Math Algorithms Geometry

This project explores the fascinating world of fractal geometry, particularly focusing on the Mandelbrot set. I developed algorithms to generate and visualize fractals using Python, while also studying their mathematical properties and applications in nature, art, and computer science.

Project Components

  • Interactive fractal explorer application
  • Mathematical explanation of iterative functions
  • Visual comparisons between mathematical and natural fractals
  • Analysis of fractal dimensions and self-similarity
Teacher's Feedback:

"An impressive blend of mathematical theory and computational practice. The project demonstrates sophisticated understanding of complex mathematical concepts through elegant visual representations."
